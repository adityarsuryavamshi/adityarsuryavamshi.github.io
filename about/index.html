ðŸ‘‹ Hello, I’m Aditya!

I spend a lot of time thinking about technology, business, strategy, and the intersection between them. More specifically I’m interested in how strategic choices influence technology and to some extent how technological choices once made become constraints for strategy. I’m fortunate enough to have people around me who are also interested in some of these things and we have fun conversing about these things. A bunch of the ideas that I write about would either have originated from those conversations or would have been in some shape or form influenced by them.

I have two primary reasons for this blog. The first is primarily for me to write down some of the commonly occurring themes and concepts that I have noticed and think about so that they can be used as a reference or building blocks in the future. And the second reason which is more of hope is that the contents of the blog will lead to/will be extensions of the conversation I have so much fun having with my friends.

As such I would love to hear from you and if you have anything to say please feel free to contact me. I’m yet to set up a mailbox on this domain, but until then you can DM me on X/Twitter at @_adityars.

I hope to write regularly and if you want to be notified of new stuff, you can subscribe to RSS feed like the good old days.

This blog is built using Hugo with Inky theme and hosted on Github pages.

Hope you have a great rest of the day!
